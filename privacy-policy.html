Privacy Policy

Last updated: 24 June 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Stephen Akintayo Foundation and SACI Holdings (“we”, “us”, or “our”) collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit the Real Estate Business Masterclass (RBM) website or register for the programme.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Registration details — such as your first name, last name, email address, phone number, occupation or role, and how you heard about the programme when you submit our registration form.
  • Payment information — when you enroll, payments are processed by Stripe. We do not store your full card details on our servers; Stripe handles payment data in accordance with its own privacy policy.
  • Communications — information you provide when you contact us by email or phone.
  • Usage and device data — such as pages visited, browser type, IP address, and general interaction data collected through analytics tools like the Meta (Facebook) Pixel.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process your registration and enrollment in the masterclass
  • Send programme updates, reminders, and support related to your participation
  • Respond to enquiries and provide customer support
  • Improve our website, marketing, and programme experience
  • Measure the effectiveness of our advertising campaigns
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations

3. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party providers to operate this website and deliver the programme. These may include:

  • Formspree — to process registration form submissions
  • Stripe — to process secure online payments
  • Meta (Facebook) — for analytics and advertising measurement via the Meta Pixel
  • Google Fonts — to display website typography

These providers may process your data according to their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review their policies for more information.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website may use cookies, pixels, and similar technologies to understand how visitors use the site and to support our marketing efforts. You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect certain features of the website.

5. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information only:

  • With service providers who help us operate the website and deliver the programme
  • When required by law or to protect our legal rights
  • With your consent, where applicable

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes described in this policy, including programme delivery, support, record-keeping, and legal compliance.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. International Users

The Real Estate Business Masterclass serves participants globally. If you access this website from outside Nigeria, your information may be transferred to and processed in countries where our service providers operate.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information, subject to legal obligations
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us using the details below.

10. Children’s Privacy

This programme and website are not directed at children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated “Last updated” date. Continued use of the website after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
